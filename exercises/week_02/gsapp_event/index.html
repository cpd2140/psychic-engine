Graphics Project is an annual series of lectures, discussions, and
portfolio reviews exploring the role of graphic design within the field
of architecture organized by Yoonjai Choi.

We examine various methods of visual communication used to
convey concepts to both specialist and general audiences. These
events aim to help students build a successful graduation portfolio
while simultaneously unpacking the topics, tools, and trends of
contemporary graphic design. Past workshop recordings and
additional resources are available on the Graphics Project Webpage.

Graphics Project Workshops are open to all current GSAPP
